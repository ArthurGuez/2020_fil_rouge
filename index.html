Agency

There's something about great design that allows it to go practically unnoticed: doing less accomplishes more.

Since it doesn't take much to make things confusing and frustrating, we believe minimalist design achieves more by making room for valuable content. Contrast Agency shines a light on why it’s important to apply this philosophy to your website. Our goal is to help your audience focus on the things that matter while removing everything that distracts them from your message.

Minimalism and sustainability go hand in hand. They share common resolutions. Since both concepts overlap, it just reinforces their validity.

Moreover, Contrast has at heart to provide you with a website displaying meaningful content perceivable by everyone. Good design means sufficient contrast between foreground and background colors. Accessibility has many benefits and in the end everyone has a better user experience with an improved layout and design. If it's important enough to be seen, then it needs to be clear.

We think Einstein said it best: “Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler”.

Concept

The contrast ratio is calculated by comparing the relative luminance values of two colors. This ranges from 1:1 for the same color, or no contrast, to 21:1 for white against black, the strongest contrast.


Color is a large part of visual design but using color to convey meaning is not a good practice. Indeed, while some people need high contrast, some people are sensitive to brightness and need to be able to change the colors. As for those who do not have specific visual conditions, If your content works in different lighting conditions, such as sunlight and glare, it is easier to read for them as well.


Making your content accessible by everyone, including those who do not have specific visual conditions, is something that we care about. Every website should allow people to change the color of text and background, and web pages need to work when people change colors. At Contrast Agency we believe this should be done during the early design stage and the selection of color palettes.


With good colors, websites and applications are easier to use in any situation, by anyone.
